Keeping content and appearance separate: tips and examples

We have two different technologies for content and the way it looks: HTML and CSS. The idea behind these is that the two different roles are kept separate using the two languages.

If you have been following the advice in {{xref("html-semantic")}}, then this shouldn't be too hard. The way you write your HTML should still be about content: choose markup based on what the pieces mean and what are they for.
